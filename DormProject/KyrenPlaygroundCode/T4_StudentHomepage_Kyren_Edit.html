Comet Hall

The largest dorm building, with up to 2 floors of several different suite-style rooms, this dorm can fit up to 210 students. Room assignments consist of doubles, triples, quads and even quints. This dorm is best for people who prefer larger communities.

Auroa Hall

A 5 floor tower-style dorm that can fit up to 100 students. Room assignments consist of singles, doubles, triples and quads, where each floor consists of 8 different rooms .

Solstice Hall

Solstice hall is a 6 floor tower-style dorm that can fit up to 45 students. Room assignments consist of singles and doubles, where each floor consists of 5 different rooms. This dorm is best for people who prefer an apartment style of living and like smaller spaces.
