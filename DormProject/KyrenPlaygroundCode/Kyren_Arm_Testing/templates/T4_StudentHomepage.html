Overview:

The overview page gives you an outline of your application. Which room you have been assigned, who you're dorming with, and who your suitemates are.

Dorm Application:

The dorm application page gives you access to the application. There is a progress bar at the bottom of the page and you should get a page that says "Your response has been recorded." when you have completed the application. When you complete this application, the overview page should update with your information.
